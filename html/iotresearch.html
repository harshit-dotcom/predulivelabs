INTERNET OF THINGS


• INTRODUCTION

THE INTERNET OF THINGS (IOT) REFERS TO A SYSTEM OF INTERRELATED, INTERNET-CONNECTED OBJECTS THAT ARE ABLE TO COLLECT AND TRANSFER DATA OVER A WIRELESS NETWORK WITHOUT HUMAN INTERVENTION. IOT IS AN ADVANCED AUTOMATION AND ANALYTICS SYSTEM, WHICH DEALS WITH ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE, SENSOR, NETWORKING, ELECTRONIC, CLOUD MESSAGING ETC. TO DELIVER COMPLETE SYSTEMS FOR THE PRODUCT OR SERVICES. THE SYSTEM CREATED BY IOT HAS GREATER TRANSPARENCY, CONTROL, AND PERFORMANCE.

THE INTERNET OF THINGS HELPS PEOPLE LIVE AND WORK SMARTER, AS WELL AS GAIN COMPLETE CONTROL OVER THEIR LIVES. IN ADDITION TO OFFERING SMART DEVICES TO AUTOMATE HOMES, IOT IS ESSENTIAL TO BUSINESS.
